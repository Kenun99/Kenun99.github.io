Biography

Weimin Chen is a second-year PhD student in Department of Computing at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, under the supervision of Prof. Daniel Xiapu Luo. Before that, he received a B.S. in Cyber Security from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT) in 2021, supervised by Prof. Haoyu Wang.

His research interests span blockchain security and software engineering, especially smart contract analysis, binary translation, fuzzing, and CUDA.
