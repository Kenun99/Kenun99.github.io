General

Weimin Chen is a first-year PhD Student at PolyU, under the supervision of Dr. Xiapu Luo. Before that, He received a B.S. in Cyber Security from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications in 2021.

His research interests span blockchain security and software engineering, especially smart contract analysis, binary translation, fuzzing, and CUDA development. He has published several paper on top-tier venues (ISSTA / Sigmetrics).
